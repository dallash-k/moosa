To the one who makes ordinary moments feel like magic,

There are days when I close my eyes and replay the sound of your laughter — how it fills every corner of a room and somehow finds its way into my chest and stays there, warm and certain.

I have been trying to find the right words for a long time now. Not because I didn't know what to say, but because some feelings are so large they resist the smallness of sentences. Yet here I am, trying anyway. Because you deserve to know.

You are someone who changes the light in a room simply by walking into it. Someone who listens not just with their ears but with their whole being. Someone who makes the world feel less like a place to survive and more like a place to truly inhabit.

These photos — these small frozen moments — are proof that beauty is not found in grand gestures alone. It lives in a glance across a table. In the way you tilt your head when you're thinking. In the quiet between words where understanding lives.

I don't know what the future holds. But I know that every memory we've made together feels like something I want to carry forward — carefully, gratefully, and with my whole heart.

Thank you for existing in my world.

Always yours, With love and wonder

Alice
